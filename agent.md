# NETRUNNER CREW RECRUITMENT - Project Notes

## Theme - NIGHT CITY GANG AESTHETIC
- **Dark, gritty, dangerous** - This is the streets, not a corporate job fair
- **Neon in the darkness** - Purple, cyan, magenta cutting through black
- **Glitch/corruption** - Things breaking, systems failing
- **Underground resistance** - We're recruiting for the fight, not a club

## Visual References
- Cyberpunk 2077 netrunner terminals
- Edgerunners anime - Lucy's aesthetic
- Voodoo Boys gang visuals
- Maelstrom cyberpsychosis edge

## Color Palette
- Deep black backgrounds (#000000, #0a0a0f)
- Neon purple (#bc13fe, #9d00ff)
- Cyan (#00ffff, #00e5ff)
- Blood red accents (#ff003c)
- Sick green for terminals (#0aff0a)

## Tone - WHAT WE'RE SAYING
- "You don't belong in the system"
- "The corps will use you and discard you"
- "We offer family, protection, purpose"
- "Street kids with nothing to lose"
- "Join or get crushed"

## What We're NOT
- Not a tech company
- Not a school club
- Not a game
- Not safe, not clean, not corporate

## Pages/Sections
1. **STREET** - Hero, immediate impact
2. **CREW** - Who we are (the family you chose)
3. **STREETS** - What we do (runs, jobs, survival)
4. **LINK** - Contact (encrypted, burner only)

## Technical
- Heavy glitch effects
- CRT scanlines
- Terminal typography
- Matrix/data corruption visuals
- Responsive but mobile-first (street kids use phones)
