## HISTORY

### Origins (2074)
ZERO_COOL was founded by **Marcus "Zero" Chen**, a former Arasaka netrunner who discovered evidence of Project: Ghostwalker - a black ops program experimenting on young netrunners to create human-AI hybrids. When Marcus tried to expose it, Arasaka burned him. He disappeared into Watson, presumed dead.

Six months later, a message appeared on every public terminal in Northside Watson:
```
> SYSTEM COMPROMISED
> TRUTH UPLOADED
> ZERO_COOL IS WATCHING
```

The first data dump exposed Arasaka's human experimentation. Marcus never showed his face, but recruits came anyway - street kids, corpo refugees, anyone who'd been crushed by the machine.

### The Night Market Incident (2075)
ZERO_COOL's first public operation. Maelstrom was running a cyberware trafficking ring out of the Night Market. ZC hitters coordinated with Mox fixers to expose the operation. Result: 47 trafficked kids freed, 3 Maelstrom lieutenants arrested by NCPD (rare corpo cooperation).

**Cost:** Lost 2 crew members - "Switch" and "Neon" - captured and cyberpsychotically converted by Maelstrom. Their fate remains unknown.

### The Blackwall Contact (2076)
A ZC runner known only as "Echo" made contact with something beyond the Blackwall during a routine Arasaka intrusion. Echo returned... changed. Now speaks in riddles and code. Some say Echo is half-AI. Some say Echo is dead and something else is wearing their brain. ZC leadership keeps Echo close - a weapon or a warning, nobody knows.

### Current Status (2077)
Operating out of a abandoned subway station beneath Northside Watson. Officially doesn't exist. Unofficially:
- **47 active runners** (field operatives)
- **~200 associates** (informants, tech support, safe houses)
- **12 confirmed kills** (corpo security, rival gangs, traitors)
- **3 territories** under "protection" (Northside Market, Kabuki Blocks, Charter Hill safehouses)

## RECRUITMENT PRACTICES

### Who They Look For
- **Age:** 16+ (no younger - Moonshadow's rule)
- **Background:** Street kids, corpo refugees, anyone with a grudge
- **Skills:** Netrunning preferred, but all skills valued
- **Loyalty:** Must pass "The Test" (see below)

### The Test
Recruits are given a choice:
1. **Hack a corpo terminal** and extract specific data (netrunners)
2. **Infiltrate a gang operation** and report back (field ops)
3. **Survive 24 hours** in hostile territory with no backup (everyone)

Failure = Rejection (or death)
Success = Probationary membership

### Initiation
New members receive:
- A **neural tattoo** of the ZC symbol (glows under UV light)
- A **burner phone** with encrypted ZC network
- A **choice of handle** (old name dies with the old life)

## COMMUNICATION PROTOCOLS

- Changed weekly

- Encrypted channels, rotate every 48 hours

- **Green light in window:** Safe house active
