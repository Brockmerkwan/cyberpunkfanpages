// ORIGINS

In 2074, Marcus "Zero" Chen discovered something he wasn't supposed to. As an Arasaka netrunner, he had access to black ops files most could only dream of. What he found was Project: Ghostwalker — human experimentation to create human-AI hybrids.

When Marcus tried to expose it, Arasaka burned him. Officially, he died in a "netrunning accident." Unofficially, he disappeared into Watson with blood in his eyes and revenge in his heart.

Six months later, every public terminal in Northside Watson displayed the same message:

> SYSTEM COMPROMISED
> TRUTH UPLOADED
> ZERO_COOL IS WATCHING

The first data dump exposed Arasaka's human experimentation. Thousands of eddies worth of corpo secrets flooded the Net. Marcus never showed his face, but recruits came anyway — street kids, corpo refugees, anyone who'd been crushed by the machine.
